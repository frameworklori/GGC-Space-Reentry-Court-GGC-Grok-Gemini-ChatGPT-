# Solar Disposal & Deep Space Disposal Risks  
太陽投放 / 深空拋棄 的風險  
Space Reentry Environmental Jury · 02_Deorbit_and_Disposal

---

## 1. Overview 概述  
Some have proposed sending retired satellites:
- into heliocentric (solar) disposal trajectories  
- or far away into deep-space escape orbits  

These methods avoid Earth re-entry but introduce new risks.

---

## 2. Gemini Atmospheric Assessment  
### Pros  
- Zero atmospheric emissions  
- No Al₂O₃ pollution  

### Cons  
- Not scalable (requires large delta-v)  
- Reduces visibility for tracking  

Gemini 結論：對地球環境有利，但推動代價過高。

---

## 3. Grok Engineering Assessment  

### Solar Disposal  
- Requires 1–3 km/s delta-v  
- Fuel mass ratio too high for small satellites  
- Only feasible for large propulsion buses  

### Deep Space Disposal  
- Requires escape velocity  
- Fuel requirements 4–6 km/s delta-v  
- Not feasible for constellations  
- Creates “lost mass” risk in solar system  

工程結論：屬於「工程上幾乎不可行」的方案。

---

## 4. ChatGPT Governance Assessment  
### Governance Concerns  
- No global framework for dumping material into solar orbit  
- Sets dangerous precedent for “off-world waste”  
- Violates principles of interplanetary environmental ethics  

### Ethical View  
Just because disposal “leaves Earth” does not remove responsibility.  
This expands pollution beyond Earth’s environment.

治理結論：此方案不應鼓勵或合法化。
