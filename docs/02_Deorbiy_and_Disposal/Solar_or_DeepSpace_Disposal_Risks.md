# Solar or Deep-Space Disposal Risks  
*(English Version — Public-Friendly Edition)*

---

## 1. What Is “Solar / Deep-Space Disposal”?

Some engineers and companies have suggested:

- **“Throw retired satellites into the Sun”**, or  
- **“Push them into deep space, away from Earth”**

The idea sounds clean:
> “If we send junk far away, it won’t bother Earth.”

But in reality, **solar and deep-space disposal are not simple or risk-free**.

This document explains why.

---

## 2. Why It’s Hard to “Throw Something Into the Sun”

Intuitively, people imagine:
> “Just aim the satellite at the Sun and push.”

In orbital mechanics, this is wrong.

- Earth is orbiting the Sun at ~30 km/s.  
- Satellites in Earth orbit **share that speed**.  
- To fall into the Sun, you must **cancel most of that orbital speed**.  

That means:

- Enormous fuel requirements  
- Much more energy than going to Mars in many cases  
- Not realistic for most satellites

So “sending satellites into the Sun” is **rarely feasible**.

---

## 3. Deep-Space Disposal: “Out of Sight” vs. “Out of Control”

Deep-space disposal means:
- pushing a satellite out of Earth orbit  
- into a solar orbit or far trajectory  
- so it never comes back

Problems:

1. **Energy Cost**  
   - Requires large delta-v (fuel and mass).  
   - Not affordable for thousands of satellites.

2. **Loss of Tracking**  
   - Once far away, tracking becomes harder.  
   - Lost objects become **uncontrolled masses** in the inner solar system.

3. **Planetary Impact Risk (Very Long Term)**  
   - Over millions of years, orbits can change.  
   - Some may cross paths with other planets or valuable future orbits.

4. **Ethical Precedent**  
   - “If we don’t see it, it’s not our problem”  
   - This mindset encourages **exporting waste into the solar system** instead of managing it responsibly.

---

## 4. Environmental & Governance Risks

### ● Solar System as a Dumping Ground  
If every spacefaring nation starts throwing hardware into deep space:

- We turn near-solar space into a **permanent junk belt**  
- Future missions (scientific or human) may cross fields of old hardware  
- It becomes impossible to clean up

### ● No Clear Responsibility  
Once a satellite leaves Earth’s neighborhood:

- Which country is responsible?  
- Does liability still apply?  
- Can anyone send objects onto potentially risky orbits?

Current treaties **do not clearly address** deep-space waste.

---

## 5. Comparison: Solar/Deep-Space vs Other Disposal Options

| Method                      | Fuel Cost | Pollution on Earth Atmosphere | Long-Term Control | Governance Clarity |
|----------------------------|----------:|-------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Natural Reentry            | Low       | High (Al₂O₃, etc.)           | Low                | Partial            |
| Controlled Reentry         | Medium    | Medium (better targeting)     | Medium             | Improving          |
| Orbital Tug + Recycling    | Medium–High | Low                          | High               | To be defined      |
| High-Orbit Graveyard (GEO) | Medium    | Low for Earth’s atmosphere    | Medium             | Known              |
| Solar / Deep-Space Disposal| Very High | Low for Earth, but shifts risk to solar system | Low (once lost) | Almost none        |

Solar / deep-space disposal **reduces Earth’s atmospheric pollution**,  
but **creates new risks we cannot monitor or fix**.

---

## 6. Why This Matters for the Future

Humanity is just beginning to build a space economy.

If we set the wrong precedent now:
- future generations inherit a **solar system full of unmanaged junk**  
- deep-space science and exploration become more dangerous  
- ethical trust in space operators is damaged  

The question is not just:
> “Can we get rid of it from Earth?”

It is:
> “Are we exporting our waste problem to the rest of the solar system?”

# 太陽軌道／深空處理的風險  
*(中文版本 — 大眾閱讀版)*

---

## 1. 什麼是「太陽或深空處理」？

有些工程師或公司提出：

- 把報廢衞星 **丟向太陽**，或  
- 把衞星推離地球，**送到深空軌道**

聽起來很乾淨：
> 「東西離地球越遠越好，眼不見為淨。」

但實際上，**太陽／深空處理既不簡單，也不真正安全**。

這份文件說明原因。

---

## 2. 為什麼「丟進太陽」其實很難？

很多人直覺認為：
> 「對準太陽推過去不就好了？」

在軌道力學裡，事情完全不是這樣。

- 地球繞太陽的速度約 30 km/s  
- 地球軌道上的衞星 **同樣帶著這個速度**  
- 要掉進太陽，必須 **大幅抵消這個公轉速度**  

這意味著：

- 需要巨量燃料  
- 能量成本遠大於飛去火星的任務  
- 對大多數衞星而言根本不實際  

所以「把衞星丟進太陽」  
在工程上幾乎就是 **幻想級選項**。

---

## 3. 深空處理：眼不見，還是在那裡

深空處理的做法是：

- 把衞星推離地球軌道  
- 送上太陽繞行軌道或遠離路徑  
- 期待它「永遠不要回來」

問題是：

1. **能量成本極高**  
   - 需要很大的變軌速度（delta-v）  
   - 不適合在大量星鏈時代使用  

2. **失去追蹤**  
   - 距離遠、反射弱，追蹤變困難  
   - 變成 **無人管理的漂浮物**

3. **超長期軌道風險**  
   - 經過極長時間，軌道可能被擾動  
   - 未來可能與其他行星或任務軌道產生交會風險  

4. **倫理先例非常危險**  
   - 「看不到就不算問題」  
   - 等於把垃圾往太陽系深處推，讓後代去承擔

---

## 4. 環境與治理風險

### ● 把太陽系當成垃圾場

若所有太空國家都採用深空丟棄策略：

- 內太陽系會逐漸堆滿報廢硬體  
- 未來的科學探測、人類任務都可能穿越垃圾帶  
- 一旦規模成形，**幾乎不可能清理**

### ● 責任邊界模糊

當衞星離開地球附近之後：

- 哪個國家需要負責？  
- 有沒有「時效」？  
- 若未來造成風險算誰的？  

現行條約 **幾乎沒有明確規範深空垃圾問題**。

---

## 5. 與其他處理方式的比較

| 處理方式              | 燃料成本 | 對地球大氣污染 | 長期控制性 | 法律／治理清晰度 |
|-----------------------|---------:|----------------|------------|------------------|
| 自然再入              | 低       | 高（Al₂O₃ 等） | 低         | 部分有規範       |
| 可控再入              | 中       | 中              | 中         | 漸漸建立中       |
| 拖船＋軌道回收        | 中～高   | 低              | 高         | 尚待建構         |
| 高軌墓地軌道（GEO）   | 中       | 對地球大氣低    | 中         | 已有慣例         |
| 太陽／深空處理        | 很高     | 對地球低，但轉嫁到太陽系 | 低（漂浮失控） | 幾乎沒有規範     |

太陽／深空處理  
看似「不污染地球」，  
但實際上是 **把風險丟給整個太陽系，而且無法回收**。

---

## 6. 為什麼這與未來文明有關？

人類才剛開始建立太空文明。

如果現在就養成：

> 「解決不了，就往外丟」

的習慣，未來將出現：

- 太陽系滿是人類丟出去的廢棄物  
- 深空任務風險升高  
- 國際對太空營運者的信任下降  

問題不只是：
> 「能不能讓地球乾淨？」

而是：
> 「我們要不要把整個太陽系當成垃圾桶？」
