# Orbital Tugs & On-Orbit Recycling  
太空拖船與軌道回收  
Space Reentry Environmental Jury · 02_Deorbit_and_Disposal

---

## 1. Overview 概述  
Active removal and recycling are considered the most environmentally friendly disposal paths, reducing uncontrolled burn-up and preventing Al₂O₃ emissions.

主動回收與軌道回收是最環保的方式，能避免氧化鋁污染。

---

## 2. Gemini Atmospheric Assessment  
### Benefits to Atmosphere  
- 70–95% reduction in Al₂O₃ output  
- Prevents high-altitude aerosol buildup  
- Eliminates unpredictable burn-up chemistry  

Gemini 結論：減排效果最明顯的技術路線。

---

## 3. Grok Engineering Assessment  

### 3.1 Orbital Tugs  
Tech status:
- TRL 6–8  
- Demonstrated by Astroscale, Northrop  
- Cost: $10–20M per mission for 10 satellites  
- Delta-v: 100–200 m/s per target  
- Reliability: ~85% docking success  

### 3.2 On-Orbit Recycling  
Tech status:
- TRL 4–5  
- Requires modular satellite design  
- Needs standardized grappling interfaces  
- Can recover 50–70% structural mass  
- Could create closed-loop materials ecosystem  

工程結論：拖船成熟、軌道回收是未來的終極方案。

## 4. ChatGPT Governance Assessment  
### Governance Advantages  
- Enables accountability (traceable removal logs)  
- Forms basis for international recycling mandates  
- Can be funded via:
  - polluter-pays  
  - global space environmental fund  
  - launch licensing fees  

### Policy Proposal Directions  
- Require large constellations to fund tug fleets  
- Mandate standardized docking interfaces  
- International tracking for removed satellites  

治理結論：這是「制度可推動」的技術方案。
