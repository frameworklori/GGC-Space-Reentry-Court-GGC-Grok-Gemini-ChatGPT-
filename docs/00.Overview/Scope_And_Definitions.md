## 2. Definitions 定義  

### 2.1 Satellite Re-entry 衞星再入  
The uncontrolled or intentional atmospheric descent of an Earth-orbiting satellite.

### 2.2 Al₂O₃ Particles 氧化鋁微粒  
Nanometer-scale aerosols created when aluminum alloys burn during re-entry.

### 2.3 Tug-Assisted Removal 太空拖船回收  
Use of orbit-servicing spacecraft to de-orbit or retrieve defunct satellites.

### 2.4 Death Orbit / Graveyard Orbit 死亡軌道  
A high-altitude parking orbit used to store retired spacecraft.
