## 1. Summary 簡述
Today, more than **95% of satellites** rely on **natural decay + uncontrolled burn-up** as their end-of-life disposal method. This is the cheapest but also the most environmentally damaging option.

目前超過 **95% 衞星** 採用「自然軌道衰減 + 非控制式再入燒蝕」。  
這是最便宜、但也是環境破壞最大的方式。

# 2. Gemini Atmospheric Assessment  
大氣科學陪審員：Gemini

### 2.1 Atmospheric Impact  
- Each satellite produces ~30 kg Al₂O₃  
- Nanoparticles persist 5–10 years  
- Accumulation rate now exceeds natural dissipation rate  
- Peak aerosol concentration: 10⁶–10⁷ particles/cm³ (20–30 km)

### 2.2 Climate & Ozone  
- Heterogeneous reactions → ozone depletion  
- +0.01–0.05 W/m² radiative forcing  
- Local heating in polar vortex zones

結論：自然再入 = 最大 Al₂O₃ 汙染源。

## 3. Grok Engineering Assessment  
工程陪審員：Grok

### Why operators choose natural decay:
- Lowest delta-v (0–50 m/s)  
- Cheapest (near-zero cost after launch)  
- No additional hardware required  
- Works for large constellations (Starlink, OneWeb)

### Technical drawbacks:
- No control over burn-up location  
- 5–10% fragments survive  
- No burn-up telemetry logs  
- Creates high-altitude aerosol “belts”

工程結論：自然再入無法避免環境傷害。

## 4. ChatGPT Governance Analysis  
治理陪審員：ChatGPT

### Policy Problems:
- No international requirement to control re-entry  
- No emission reporting  
- No liability for atmospheric pollution  
- Insurance covers only debris impact, not chemical damage  
- A “free sink” exploitation of the stratosphere

### Ethical Impact:
- Atmospheric commons abused  
- Future generations bear the cost  
- Mega-constellations externalize environmental losses

治理結論：自然衰減 = 监管真空 + 伦理失衡。
