# Debris Injury & Insurance Model  
*(English Version — Public-Friendly Edition)*

---

## 1. Why Do We Need an Injury & Insurance Model?
When satellites reenter the atmosphere, most of the material burns up.  
But **5–20%** of the mass of certain satellites (tanks, titanium parts) can survive and:

- fall on houses  
- hit aircraft  
- injure people  
- damage crops or infrastructure  

Today, there is **no global compensation system** for:

- falling debris  
- airspace incidents  
- injuries or death  
- property damage  

People rely on outdated international laws from the 1970s.  
This model updates the rules for the modern era.

---

## 2. What Counts as “Debris Injury”?
A space debris incident includes:

- **Direct hit** (object strikes a person)  
- **Property strike** (house, car, building damage)  
- **Indirect injury** (fire, shockwave, toxic remnants)  
- **Aviation hazard** (aircraft forced to divert or damaged)  
- **Agricultural harm** (contaminated soil or destroyed crops)  

These scenarios require a structured compensation framework.

---

## 3. How Insurance Works Today (Simple Explanation)
Current laws:  
- Nations are responsible for their own satellites.  
- Victims must request compensation diplomatically.  
- No fast payout system exists.  
- No individual insurance is provided.  

This system is slow and unfair.

---

## 4. The LORI “No-Fault Global Compensation Pool”
To modernize space safety, we propose:

### A global compensation fund where:
- each satellite operator pays a small yearly fee  
- based on the satellite’s mass + reentry risk  
- payouts are automatic for victims  
- **no need for lawsuits**

This is similar to:
- earthquake insurance  
- airline safety funds  
- nuclear plant compensation pools  

Victims get help instantly, not years later.

---

## 5. Fee Calculation (Simple Formula)
Each satellite pays a yearly fee:

**Fee = (Mass × Risk Level × Reentry Frequency Factor)**

Risk level includes:
- material type  
- altitude  
- failure history  
- controlled vs. uncontrolled reentry  

Operators with safer satellites pay less.

---

## 6. What Compensation Covers
The fund covers:

### ✔ Medical care  
### ✔ Funeral compensation  
### ✔ Property repair  
### ✔ Land & crop loss  
### ✔ Income loss  
### ✔ Aviation safety disruptions  

A standardized global table provides fair payouts.

# 太空碎片傷害與保險模型  
*(中文版 — 大眾閱讀版)*

---

## 1. 為什麼需要「傷害與保險模型」？
衞星再入大氣層時，大部分會燒掉。  
但仍有 **5–20%** 的殘骸（例如鈦金屬、水箱）能活著掉落：

- 砸到房子  
- 擊中飛機  
- 傷到人  
- 毀壞農地或設施  

目前 **全球沒有一套統一的賠償制度**：

- 殘骸砸死人  
- 殘骸砸房子  
- 污染土地  
- 威脅飛機安全  

只能靠 1970 年代的老法律，非常落後。

---

## 2. 什麼算「太空碎片傷害」？
包括：

- **直接擊中人**  
- **砸到房屋或車輛**  
- **引發火災或毒性傷害**  
- **飛機因碎片被迫改道或受損**  
- **農地被污染或作物毀壞**  

這些情況都需要明確的賠償制度。

---

## 3. 現行制度怎麼運作？（簡單版）
目前的制度：

- 國家要為自己的衞星負責  
- 受害者要靠外交請求賠償  
- 沒有快速賠償系統  
- 也沒有給民眾的保險制度  

又慢又不公平。

---

## 4. LORI「全球無過失賠償基金」
為了建立現代化太空安全，我們提出：

### 全球共用賠償基金（Global Pool）
- 所有衞星營運者每年付一筆小額費用  
- 費用依照「質量 × 風險 × 再入頻率」計算  
- 受害者自動獲得賠償  
- **不需要打官司、不需要跨國請求**

類似：

- 地震保險  
- 航空業安全基金  
- 核能事故賠償制度  

讓受害者「立即獲得補償」。

---

## 5. 年費計算（簡易公式）
衞星每年支付基金費用：

**費用 =（質量 × 風險等級 × 再入頻率係數）**

風險等級包括：

- 材料類型  
- 軌道高度  
- 過去是否曾失控  
- 是控制再入還是不受控再入  

安全的衞星付款比較少。

---

## 6. 賠償範圍
基金提供：

### ✔ 醫療費  
### ✔ 喪葬補償  
### ✔ 財產修復  
### ✔ 農地與作物損失  
### ✔ 工作收入損失  
### ✔ 航空安全干擾賠償  

使用全球統一標準表。
