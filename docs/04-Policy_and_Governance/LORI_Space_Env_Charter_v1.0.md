# LORI Space Environmental Charter v1.0  
LORI 太空環境憲章 v1.0  
Space Reentry Environmental Jury · 04_Policy_and_Governance

---

## Preamble 前言  
As satellite constellations expand and Earth’s stratosphere becomes a new environmental frontier, all spacefaring actors must uphold the principles of human sovereignty, environmental stewardship, and intergenerational responsibility.

隨著衛星星鏈急速增加，地球大氣層正成為新的環境戰場。  
本憲章旨在限制破壞、鼓勵負責、並保護後代。

---

# Article 1 — Human Sovereignty  
**人類主權至上**

1. All AI systems act only as advisory entities.  
2. Final environmental decisions must belong to humans.  
3. No automated system may override human judgment.

---

# Article 2 — Atmospheric Responsibility  
**大氣層環境責任**

1. The stratosphere is a shared commons.  
2. No nation or corporation may pollute it without global accountability.  
3. Al₂O₃ emissions must be monitored, quantified, and reported.

---

# Article 3 — Clean Satellite Standards  
**乾淨衛星標準**

1. Operators must transition away from aluminum-heavy designs.  
2. Clean materials list shall be periodically updated.  
3. Re-entry plans must minimize aerosol formation.

---

# Article 4 — End-of-Life Responsibility  
**生命週期責任**

1. Every satellite must have a disposal plan.  
2. Natural uncontrolled re-entry should be phased out.  
3. Tug-assisted removal or recycling shall be prioritized.

---

# Article 5 — Governance & Consent  
**治理與授權**

1. Large constellations require multi-country consent.  
2. Launch licensing must include environmental impact assessment (Space-EIA & RE-EIA).  
3. Operators must contribute to the Global Space Environmental Fund (GSEF).

---

# Article 6 — Accountability  
**責任與追究**

1. All satellites must include traceability modules.  
2. Operators are strictly liable for debris injury.  
3. Emission charges shall reflect atmospheric damage.

---

# Article 7 — Transparency  
**透明化**

1. Annual public reporting of:  
   - total re-entry mass  
   - Al₂O₃ injection  
   - debris recovery  
2. Open data sharing among nations.

---

# Article 8 — Enforcement  
**執行機制**

1. Violations may result in:  
   - launch suspension  
   - financial penalties  
   - international sanctions  
2. Enforcement authority determined by treaty-signing members.
