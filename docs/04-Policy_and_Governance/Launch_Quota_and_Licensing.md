## 1. Background 背景  
The world currently has **no global limits** on:
- number of satellites launched  
- yearly re-entry mass  
- total Al₂O₃ injection  
- debris risk contribution  

全球不存在任何「衛星發射量」的國際上限。  
每個國家只管自己，不管大氣累積污染。

# 2. Gemini Atmospheric Assessment  
大氣科學陪審員：Gemini

### Atmospheric Rationale for Quotas  
Reducing:
- total re-entry frequency → reduces Al₂O₃ loading  
- orbital crowding → reduces debris risks  
- atmospheric injection → slows ozone damage  

### Recommended Maximums  
- Global re-entry mass: **≤ 50,000 kg/year**  
- Global Al₂O₃ injection: **≤ 200,000 kg/year**  
- Global launch count: **≤ 5,000 satellites/year**

Gemini 結論：需要全球量化上限。

# 3. Grok Engineering Assessment  
工程陪審員：Grok

### Engineering Basis for Launch Licensing  
A licensing system could require:
- End-of-life (EOL) disposal plan  
- EOL fuel reserve  
- material breakdown report  
- burn-up simulation file  
- risk assessment file  
- debris identification module  
- international tracking number (similar to ICAO aircraft ID)

### Engineering Rationale  
Operators must prove they will not exceed:
- pollution limits  
- orbital congestion thresholds  
- collision probability standards  

工程結論：發射許可制完全可行。

# 4. ChatGPT Governance Assessment  
治理陪審員：ChatGPT

### Policy Structure  
Launch licensing should require:
1. **Environmental Impact Assessment (Space-EIA)**  
2. **Re-entry Environmental Impact Assessment (RE-EIA)**  
3. **Quota check**  
4. **Materials compliance report**  
5. **Liability coverage**  
6. **Contribution to Global Space Environmental Fund (GSEF)**  

### Legal Framework  
Can be implemented under:
- UN Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space (COPUOS)  
- New Space Environmental Agency  
- Multinational treaty  
- LORI Space Ethics Charter  

治理結論：制度成熟、可以落地。
