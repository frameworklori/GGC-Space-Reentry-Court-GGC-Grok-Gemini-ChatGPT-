# Rocket & Re-entry Emissions  
火箭排放與再入燒蝕排放  
Space Reentry Environmental Jury · 01_Atmospheric_Damage

---

## 1. Types of Emissions 排放類型  
### 1.1 Launch Emissions  
- CO₂, H₂O vapor  
- Black carbon soot  
- Alumina (from solid rocket motors)  
- NOx  

### 1.2 Re-entry Emissions  
Produced through hypersonic ablation:
- Al₂O₃ nanoparticles  
- TiO₂  
- MgO, FeO traces  
- NOx from thermal dissociation  

再入大氣層時產生的污染物通常被低估，但其影響更集中在高空層。

---

## 2. Gemini Atmospheric Analysis  
### 2.1 Launch Contribution  
- Black carbon at 30–40 km → strong radiative forcing  
- Water vapor at 40–60 km → promotes PSC-like chemistry  

### 2.2 Re-entry Contribution  
Relative impact:
- **Launch = short-lived pollution (days–months)**  
- **Re-entry = long-lived aerosols (years)**  

結論：長期氣候影響主要來自再入，而非發射。

## 3. Grok Engineering Insights  
- Solid rocket boosters produce alumina, but SpaceX liquid engines produce less  
- Re-entry heating determines oxidation completeness  
- Fragment survival increases debris liability risk  

工程結論：發射污染可技術減少，再入難以避免。
