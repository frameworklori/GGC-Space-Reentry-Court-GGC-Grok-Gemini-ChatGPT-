## 1. Problem 問題來源  
Currently, no country or organization is responsible for:
- detecting Al₂O₃ buildup  
- tracking aerosol distribution  
- verifying satellite operator emissions  
- reporting stratospheric chemical changes  

完全沒有全球監管大氣污染物累積的單位。

---

## 2. Gemini Atmospheric Proposal  
### Multi-layer monitoring model  
- **LIDAR arrays** measuring aerosol profiles  
- **High-altitude balloons** sampling micro-particles  
- **Satellite spectrometers** detecting radiative forcing patterns  
- **GOSAT-GW network** for long-term CO₂ + aerosol tracking  

Proposed thresholds:
- Stratospheric Al₂O₃ ≥ 50 ng/m³ → warning  
- ≥ 200 ng/m³ → mandatory international review

## 4. ChatGPT Governance Design  
### Responsibility Assignment  
A global model:
- Spacefaring nations share baseline monitoring  
- Operators pay monitoring fees (polluter-pays principle)  
- UN or a new agency publishes annual “Space Environmental Report”  

Governance principles:
- Transparency  
- Attribution  
- Accountability  
- Human sovereignty
